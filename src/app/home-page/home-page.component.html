THEME "ECO- INNOVATION FOR ENVIRONMENTAL SUSTAINABILITY"

"ECO INNOVATION" means to pursue knowledge and practices that can lead to more environmentally friendly and ecologically responsible decisions and lifestyles, which can help protect the environment and sustain its natural resources for current and future generations in NIGERIA.
